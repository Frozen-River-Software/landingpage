Accessibility

Frozen River Software aims to make this website usable by as many people as possible. We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines where practical and to continuously improve based on feedback.

Our approach

  • Semantic HTML, clear headings, and keyboard-accessible controls
  • Visible focus styles and sufficient color contrast
  • Reduced motion support via prefers-reduced-motion
  • Descriptive labels, alt text, and ARIA where needed

Known limitations

  • Some visual enhancements may not be fully navigable by screen readers
  • Experimental animations are disabled when reduced motion is requested

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility issue, please email info@frozenriversoftware.com with a description and your browser/device. We’ll do our best to address it promptly.
