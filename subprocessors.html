Subprocessors

This page lists the third‑party providers we use to operate this website and communications. These providers may process limited personal data (e.g., your email address if you subscribe to updates).

  • Buttondown — newsletter/email delivery (stores subscriber email addresses)
  • Vercel — website hosting (serves pages; may log IP/user agent)
  • Cloudflare — DNS/CDN (may process request logs)
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (may log minimal request data)

For product features, additional processors may be used and documented within those products or in future updates of this page.
